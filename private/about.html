The best travel company for Sri Lanka holidays

We are delighted to introduce our exciting venture “Travel Ease Holidays”, operating as a full service Destination Management & Outbound Travel Company providing comprehensive services for groups and individuals from and to the country.

Travel Ease Holidays is a travel agent and a tour operator based in Sri Lanka, with an additional office in London, specializing in providing tailor-made Sri Lanka tours, outbound holiday packages, hotel bookings and ground transportation arrangements for global inbound & outbound tourism and is therefore a one- stop enterprise that offers you, our esteemed clients, a complete range of travel related services.

We also offer innovative and cost-effective travel management solutions to the corporate market.

Our dedicated team has gathered exceptional experience over the years within the travel trade which helps us offer our clients the chance to enjoy booking a hassle free travel experience.

Our aim is to meet our client’s expectations along with high quality and personalized services, reliability and flexibility, in accordance with the best rates.

We work closely with hotels, airlines and local/foreign suppliers to offer you the very best rates and ensure you can book with the utmost confidence as we are readily contactable and available locally to resolve any potential problems along the way. Our travel consultants are equipped with superior local knowledge, efficient planning skills, along with experience in dealing with all types of clients. These are just some of the reasons behind our success.
