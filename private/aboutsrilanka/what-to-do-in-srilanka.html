About Sri Lanka

What to do in Sri Lanka

Explore the many magical corners of paradise island, as you tour Sri Lanka - the pearl of Asia. Experience an adventure like never before as your wildest fantasies come to life in this truly diverse land of opportunity, adventure, exploration and relaxation. From specialist tours that bring you closer to nature and the island’s cultural diversity; to tailor-made packages fit for royalty, revel in the beauty of paradise, your way.

Whether a spiritual journey that connects you to mother earth; promoting the balance of mind, body and soul; find your inner peace amidst the rolling hills of Ella or Nuwara Eliya, or find yourself within the tropical forests of Sinharaja as your meditate to the symphony of the earth. Traverse time, as you make paradise island the backdrop to your honeymoon adventure. Experience a growing connection as you embark on a journey of togetherness. From luxury resort holidays, created to pamper rejuvenate to an explorers paradise across Sri Lanka’s cultural triangle.

Be at one, with the serenity of Sri Lanka’s central highlands, as you become part of a world once forgotten. From expansive tea estates, set to the backdrop of rolling mountains, misty trails, colourful gardens and colonial architecture, let the adventurer in you take over. Enjoy the relaxing countryside bliss or experience the natural trails and mountain hikes that Sri Lanka has become popularly known for.

From palm-fringed golden beaches to deep-sea diving, adrenaline junkies can enjoy the best of water sports and water activities that an island has to offer. Find your blissful slice of golden coast along the 370k eastern coastline or 250km western coastline of Sri Lanka. From jet-skiing to kite surfing; dolphin watching to cave exploration, paradise island becomes your home for the summer.

Make memories amongst Sri Lanka’s natural offering, as you come up close and personal with rare animals and flora. From herds of elephants, nature’s gentle giants; to the elusive leopard; to the mischievous group of monkeys - nature’s natural entertainers; and more.

The perfect avenue for family holidays and relaxing getaways, make Sri Lanka your destination for this coming holiday.
