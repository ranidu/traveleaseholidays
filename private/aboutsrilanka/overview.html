About Sri Lanka

Overview

Experience a symphony of travel, like never before. Embark on a journey that will light the fires of adventure, stroke the inferno of knowledge, rekindle the flames of romance or restore the weary spirit.

Set as the Pearl of the Indian Ocean, Sri Lanka offers a memorable experience, welcoming all-walks of travellers, offering them a haven to rest, relax and encounter a truly, one-of-a-kind lifestyle.

Sri Lanka is home to lush valleys, mystical waterfalls and paddy fields, acres of tea and a coastline of golden beaches that meet the azure blue waters of the Indian Ocean. Experience the beauty of tropical luxury, no matter the season, as Sri Lanka caters to holiday-goers all through the year. From discovering Sri Lanka’s authentic cuisine brought to life by unimaginable flavour and deep colours, to travelling across the path less taken - welcome to a land of adventure, relaxation and hospitality, complemented by a rich cultural heritage. Sri Lanka offers an explorers paradise for those seeking a meaningful escape to a land home to misty mountains, sunny coastlines and a metropolis of luxury and lights.

Sri Lanka’s cultural depth is recognized by UNESCO, which has declared seven World Heritage Sites within the island. The sacred city of Anuradhapura; the medieval city of Polonnaruwa; the cave temple of Dambulla; the ancient rock fortress of Sigiriya; the sacred city of Kandy; the colonial town of Galle and its fortifications; and the Sinharaja Forest Reserve, a wilderness sanctuary offering an array of sought after endemic flora and fauna.

Experience the best of tropical travel and tourism in Sri Lanka.

Indulge in an unforgettable, luxury experience, as we breathe life into your most daringly opulent dreams.
