Authentic Sri Lankan experiences

Book a spectacular tour package through us . Whether an authentic topical experience, a romantic honeymoon, an expedition of exploration and adventure or spiritual retreat, choose from a wide variety of speciality tour packages.

Holiday Types

Multi Centre holidays

Visit complementary or contrasting destinations as you create your own unique itinerary around Sri Lanka, allowing you to experience the true essence of luxury island living, exactly as you’d want to. Combine several destinations whether beachfront, hill top, country-side, new Colombo City or tropical rainforest, to name a few and enjoy an experience an adventure around Sri Lanka.

Let us tailor-make your dream combination of destinations, hotels and tours.

MICE Travel

Whether meetings, incentives, conferences or corporate events, experience a tailor-made, personalised retreat, event option, VIP hospitality, and exclusive tour packages. Catering toward creating a long lasting impression for our MICE travellers, we provide the expertise to manage all forms of corporate forums, workshops or meeting.

Destination Weddings

Your perfect wedding destination, awaits. Choose from spectacularly magnificent hotel and event venues in Sri Lanka to host your dream wedding. Whether on the coast, in the countryside, or the heart of the city, we offer breath-taking locations, set to a backdrop of decadence, beauty and the world’s most mesmerizing sunsets.

Choose from an array of event spaces suitable for intimate ceremonies or grand affairs; in lush gardens or beachside villas; international, 5-star hotels or charming countryside weddings.

Honeymoon destination

Spend your honeymoon traversing paradise island, hand in hand with your soulmate; or spend an awe-inspiring morning swimming amidst exotic sea-life that are the envy of the most beautiful rainbows; wake up to the sight of a pod of whales or dolphins off the golden coast of Sri Lanka. Make every moment remarkable as you experience a spectacular sunrise through the misty mountain tops of Sri Lanka’s hill country or encounter rare species of wildlife. Let the romantic in you take control as you enjoy the serenity and privacy of some of Sri Lanka and the Maldives most alluring and opulent honeymoon destinations, exclusively created for you.

Academic Tours

Welcome to the perfect partnership for those seeking Educational/ Academic Tours around beautiful Asia. Welcome to a nation, rich in natural culture and heritage, that offers a plethora of historical and educational focused avenues.

Whether an architecturally inspired trip that leads you around Colonial and Victorian Sri Lanka down the path of Geoffrey and Bewis Bawa; or through the ruins of Asia lost, explore vivid British, Portuguese, Spanish and Dutch influences in art and infrastructure; if you’re looking for meetings/ lectures at acclaimed local universities with specialist professors, we cater to it all.

Welcome to a haven of knowledge and innovation, unbound.

Ladies Trips

Book your exclusive, tailor-made holiday package here. Whether a relaxing getaway to laze under the sun and take in the surf; meditation under the stars that leads you closer to finding your true self; or hiking tranquil nature trails to mystical waterfalls, water holes and untouched lush gardens.

Adventure Packages

Exploration or travel with a certain degree of risk as you experience the ‘road less traveled’. Experience the best of Sri Lanka as you take on its culture, festivities, exploration of ruins, adrenaline activities, such as mountaineering, trekking, bungee jumping, mountain biking, cycling, white water rafting, scuba diving, kayaking, zip-lining, paragliding, hiking, caving diving, rock climbing and more.

Family Holidays

This holiday, relax on a beach with a king coconut in hand as you work on the perfect tan. Experience the cool blue waters of the Indian Ocean, go on safari in Yala, snorkel amongst colourful sea-life or cruise with your loved ones around the coast to see the complete expanse of the sea. Hike through wilderness as you look for rare butterflies, animals or birds. A world of fun awaits. Make memories with your loved ones, only as you could, once in paradise island. Select from our family packages, or create your own tailor-made tour across beautiful Sri Lanka.

All Inclusive Packages

Make your dreams come true with inspiring journeys of discovery throughout Sri Lanka. From spectacular beachfront resorts to mist-surrounded central highland bungalows, experience an all inclusive holiday for the conscientious travellers. Explore your options in paradise island.

Spiritual Tours

Enjoy a spiritual journey that connects you to mother earth; promoting the balance of mind, body and soul; find your inner peace amidst the rolling hills of Ella or Nuwara Eliya, or find yourself within the tropical forests of Sinharaja as your meditate to the symphony of the earth. Bring yourself closer to the meaning of being, as you allow your soul to transcend time and space on your spiritual journey through Sri Lanka. Select from our number of spiritual tours available.

Beachfront Holidays

Home to a beautiful array of golden beaches, crashing waves, misty mountains, majestic beasts, mountain estates, country charm and warm hospitality.

With many sites and scenes wrapped up in the island nation of Sri Lanka, holiday-goers both international and local have made Sri Lanka their dream destination.

The beauty of sun, sand and surf awaits, like none can imagine. Sri Lanka becomes your go to destination for a holiday in the sun, whether for rest and relaxation or to appease the adrenaline junkie in you.
